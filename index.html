Meet Our Baker

Steph

Steph started baking in her free time at a young age and very quickly discovered a passion for all things baked. After watching every season on The Great British Bake-Off and Cake Boss, Steph decided that she would attempt to make it her full-time job. Now she is the founder and lead baker of Spiker & Sponge cakes!
